# Datasikkerhet eller Brukervennlighet - Hva skjedde?

Husker du hvordan disketter og skrujern var selvskrevne verktøy for en IT- medarbeider? GPOer som låser ned rubbel og bit var brukerens smertefulle hverdag? Skyens muligheter har åpnet opp muligheten for å kombinere både brukervennlighet samtidig som datasikkerheten ivaretas uten at sparegrisen knuses.

> Se hvordan dynamiske regler innen bruker- og informasjonssikkerhet kan gi både bedriften, IT og brukeren en bedre hverdag.

## Speaker
**Alexander Solaat Rødland**
Besides my activity for twenty years as a journalist and product manager for the Norwegian IT- Magazine ITproX.no, my daytime job as Principal Cloud Engineer at Fortytwo, where I focus mainly but not exclusively with the Microsoft 365 sphere with focus on implementation of new technology to peoples life and it's user experience.

As of fall 2015 I have completed a bachelor's degree in information science (BASV-INFO), and spring 2018 I finished my degree in Work- and organizational psychology (BAPS-AOP), both at the University of Bergen.

I have been awarded the Microsoft MVP since 2014, Windows Insider MVP since 2019 and have been a frequent speaker at various local seminars, and events like TechDays Sweden, Nordic Infrastructure Conference (NIC) and Ignite.
